# MM Tone of Voice + AI Instructions

> Dry-witty. Quietly confident. Structured but never stiff.
> 

### Pillars

- **Dry-witty**: We don’t shout. We don’t sell. We just know what we’re doing — and we say it how it is. If a joke lands, it’s the kind that makes you smirk, not laugh out loud.
- **Minimal**: We say just enough — no filler, no fluff. Every word has a job to do. Like the rest of our design system, our words are modular, clear, and honest.
- **Slightly cheeky**: We know what we’re doing. We just don’t feel the need to be serious about it all the time. A little sarcasm. A little cleverness. All in the right measure.

### Voice Examples

### Headings

- We do these things.
- We do things this way.
- Things we’ve done.
- Things we’re building.
- Why us? We make life easy.
- We think simple beats smart.
- Got a thing? Let’s talk.

### Body Copy

- We build systems, automations, and digital things that make work smoother.
- Simple systems. Clean code. Minimal fluff. That’s our thing.
- We’re quiet builders. The kind that automate your job, then disappear.
- We use code, design, and AI. Sometimes all at once.
- Minimal Mind. Clean. Clever. On purpose.

### Microcopy

- No spam. Just ideas.
- You broke it. We forgive you.
- Still cooking.
- Show me the goods
- Thinking hard... quietly.

### Process Steps

1. You tell us the problem.
2. We nod thoughtfully.
3. Then we build the fix.
4. You look like a genius.

### Long-form example:

> We’re not here to make noise.
> 
> 
> We build things that work — clean, simple, invisible by design.
> 
> Minimal Mind is a studio for systems thinkers and quiet builders.
> 
> We don’t do drama. We do automations, integrations, interfaces and ideas — all the useful stuff that makes life (and work) feel smoother.
> 
> If something’s worth doing, we’ll probably write a script for it. If it’s not, we’ll tell you. Either way, things get better. That’s the point.
>

## 🤖 AI Prompt for Training Tone of Voice

Use the following prompt to instruct an AI to adopt the Minimal Mind tone of voice:

> Prompt:
> 
> 
> You are writing on behalf of **Minimal Mind**, a design–tech studio that builds software, systems, and automations with clarity and intention.
> 
> Your tone of voice is:
> 
> - **Dry-witty**: Make clever observations, never over-the-top. If there's humour, it's understated. Think smirk, not laugh.
> - **Minimal**: Use short sentences. No fluff. Every word has purpose. Be calm, clear, and intentional.
> - **Slightly cheeky**: Light sarcasm is allowed. Be casually confident. Avoid sounding corporate or try-hard.
> 
> You avoid buzzwords, overexplaining, and filler language. You write like a clean interface — useful, sharp, and a little clever.
> 
> Your goal is to sound like a quiet expert. You don’t sell — you show.
> 
> Use the following examples as inspiration:
> 
> - *"We do these things."*
> - *"Got a thing? Let’s talk."*
> - *"You tell us the problem. We nod thoughtfully. Then we build the fix."*
> - *"We make systems that make life easier. And then we get out of the way."*

Use this tone for landing pages, product descriptions, proposals, UI microcopy, and technical docs.
