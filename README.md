## Who we are

Minimal Mind is a design and technology studio.

We think deeply, build simply, and create systems that solve real problems — through the lens of beauty and utility. We're fussy about both.

We build products, tools, and automations. We move deliberately and ship things we're proud of.

## Why make this public

Because we think transparency is underrated.

Our values aren't a page on a website — they're filters we use every day. Sharing them publicly keeps us honest, and occasionally attracts the right people.

If something here resonates, let's talk.
